UnitedHealth Medical Policy

Commercial · Medical Coverage Determination

MRI Brain — CPT 70553

Coverage: MRI of the brain (CPT 70553) is covered when ordered for evaluation of persistent headache after clinical examination. No prior conservative therapy documentation is required.

Effective date: 2026-01-01. CARC 50 applies only to clearly cosmetic indications. Policy ID: UHC-RAD-70553.
